INFORMATION MANAGEMENT IN LIBRARY

You probably think that this course is about becoming a librarian, but on this page, I will talk about the details of my course.

Taking this subject made everyone think that I want to be a librarian when I grow up. The truth is, I can be anything I want because this course can go in many ways. This branch of information management deals with the organization of information resources in a library. It involves the creation of catalogues, classification systems, and other tools for organizing and retrieving information. This branch of information management deals with the process of locating and retrieving information resources. It involves the efficient organization of information resources and the use of databases, indexes, and other tools for finding and accessing information.
